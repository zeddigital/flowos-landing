❓ FAQ

Common questions

How does FlowOS work? ↓
FlowOS is a cloud-based web platform — no app download required. You sign in on any phone, tablet, or computer. Add your customers and their devices once, then when you complete a test on site the official water authority certificate is generated automatically and sent directly to the customer. Everything is stored securely in the cloud.
Does FlowOS generate the actual official water authority certificate? ↓
Yes — FlowOS fills the exact Backflow Test Report form used by Greater Western Water, South East Water, Yarra Valley Water, and other authorities. We use the original PDF template programmatically — the layout, logos, and field positions are identical to the paper version. Nothing is recreated or approximated. The output is accepted by all major water authorities.
Can I use it on my phone at the job site? ↓
Yes — FlowOS is fully mobile responsive and works on any smartphone, tablet, or laptop browser. Most plumbers complete their test forms on a phone or tablet while standing in front of the device. The form is guided and validates your inputs in real time so you can't miss required fields.
Can multiple technicians use the same account? ↓
The Professional plan supports up to 5 technicians. Each technician gets their own login, and you can see all of their jobs, tests, and certificates from the main dashboard. Enterprise plans support unlimited technicians with role-based access control.
How do annual reminders work? ↓
FlowOS tracks the test date for every device on every property. A configurable number of days before each device's annual test falls due, FlowOS automatically sends an email and/or SMS reminder to the property contact. You can customise the message template, timing, and which customer contacts receive the reminder.
Can I import my existing customers and devices? ↓
Yes. You can import customers and devices via CSV, or our support team can assist with migration from spreadsheets or your current system. Professional and Enterprise customers get white-glove onboarding at no extra charge — we set everything up with you in a call.
Is my data safe and backed up? ↓
All data is hosted in Australian data centres (AWS Sydney region) and backed up continuously with point-in-time recovery. Every certificate is stored permanently — you can retrieve any document from any device at any time. We use AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS in transit. FlowOS is SOC 2 Type II compliant.
